OxBus: About

What is OxBus?

Oxbus is a lightweight mobile site to get live bus times for Oxfordshire bus stops near you.

Who made OxBus?

Me, Matthew Owen

How does it work?

Geolocation uses the geo-location-javascript framework.

Nearby bus stops are found using the recently(ish) released database of NAPTAN bus stops.

Buses for those stops are found by scraping the Oxfordshire live bus times website
