What is OxBus?

Oxbus is a lightweight mobile site to get live bus times for Oxfordshire bus stops near you.

Who made OxBus?

Me, Matthew Owen

  • Email
  • Twitter
  • LinkedIn

How does it work?

Geolocation uses the geo-location-javascript framework.

Nearby bus stops are found using the pretty useful NaPTAN database of public transport stops.

Buses for those stops are found by scraping the 'official' Oxfordshire live bus times website (Oxontime)
